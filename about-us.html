About Us

Tropical Resort was born in 2008 on the secluded shores of Puerto Rico’s southwest coast, when founder Andrew Ramirez—a lifelong traveler, conservationist, and proud Boricua—discovered an untouched stretch of coastline near Cabo Rojo. Determined to share its beauty without compromising its magic, he envisioned a resort that celebrated the island’s natural splendor while offering world-class hospitality. Working with local artesanos, the first ten villas were built by hand using sustainable materials like reclaimed mahogany from hurricane-felled trees and coral stone quarried in Aguadilla. Today, what began as Andrew’s passion project has blossomed into a Top 10 Sustainable Resort (Travel + Leisure, 2023), still family-run and rooted in its original mission: to protect paradise while inviting guests to experience Puerto Rico’s wonders.

Escape to Tropical Resort, where golden sands meet swaying palm trees and the ocean sparkles under endless blue skies. Designed as a sanctuary of serenity, our resort blends modern luxury with the untouched beauty of its surroundings. Whether you're lounging in a private cabana, indulging in a seaside massage, or exploring vibrant marine life just steps from your suite, every moment here feels like paradise.

Our passion is crafting unforgettable experiences—be it a sunrise yoga session on the beach, a gourmet dinner under the stars, or an adventurous kayak tour through hidden lagoons. Committed to sustainability, we work closely with local communities to protect the natural wonders that make this place so special. At Tropical Resort, we don’t just offer a getaway; we invite you to immerse yourself in the rhythm of island life, where time slows down and every detail is designed for your bliss.
