Your Escape,
The Heart of Our World

It’s the little things that transform a vacation into an unforgettable experience.
The thrill of gliding across crystal-clear waters on a paddleboard,
the excitement of exploring hidden coves by kayak,
the joy of discovering vibrant coral reefs while snorkeling.
Every moment is crafted to create memories that last a lifetime.
Welcome to Tropic Resort, where adventure and relaxation meet in paradise.

Sky Activities

We ensure every moment in the air is unforgettable. Whether it’s guiding you through the skies on a thrilling zip-lining experience or helping you feel weightless during a parasailing session, we craft adventures that reflect your sense of wonder and excitement. At Tropic, the sky is not the limit, it’s just the beginning.

Land Activities

From guiding an exhilarating hiking adventure, we tailor to your sense of adventure and relaxation. Whether it’s enjoying a sunset horseback ride along the beach to arranging a peaceful yoga session amidst the serene beauty of nature, we craft experiences that connect you to the heart of the island. At Tropic, the land is your playground, and every step is a new discovery.

Aquatic Activites

We create aquatic adventures that reflect our love for the sea. Exploring vibrant coral reefs while snorkeling, to the thrill of riding the waves during an exhilarating jet ski adventure, we dedicate every moment to being filled with excitement and wonder. At Tropic the ocean is yours to conquer, and every splash is a memory in the making.
